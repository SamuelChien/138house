A small history of our creative team

138House, located on the busy street of Cau Giay, Hanoi, was founded in 2012 by Santo Chien with a simple mission: redefine real estate in the customer's favor. Everyone told us to run ads like a traditional brokers, but we couldn’t stop thinking about how different real estate would be if it were designed from the ground up, using technology to put customers first.
